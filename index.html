I'm very closed with my parents and all of my siblings especially with my youngest brother since he lived with me and my husband. I think my family tree is unique because I have a cross-cultural extended family. I have uncles and aunts (from my father side) with a different religious beliefs and cultures which are Christian, Buddhist, Hindu and Pagan.
